Our Services

Comprehensive aircraft services to keep your fleet in optimal condition

Aircraft Repairs

Our comprehensive aircraft repair services cover all aspects of maintenance and repair for commercial, private, and military aircraft. With state-of-the-art facilities and certified technicians, we ensure your aircraft meets all safety and performance standards.

Our Repair Capabilities

  • Structural repairs and modifications
  • Engine maintenance and overhaul
  • Avionics systems repair and upgrade
  • Landing gear maintenance
  • Composite material repairs
  • Corrosion prevention and treatment

Certifications

We hold certifications from major aviation authorities including FAA, EASA, and Transport Canada, ensuring our work meets the highest international standards.

Part Repairs

Our part repair services extend the life of your aircraft components while maintaining the highest safety standards. We specialize in repairing a wide range of aircraft parts, from simple components to complex systems.

Part Repair Services

  • Hydraulic component repair
  • Pneumatic system components
  • Electrical system parts
  • Flight control components
  • Instrumentation and gauges
  • Fuel system components

Quality Assurance

All repaired parts undergo rigorous testing and quality control procedures to ensure they meet or exceed original equipment manufacturer specifications.

Aircraft Management

Our aircraft management services provide comprehensive support for aircraft owners and operators, handling all aspects of aircraft operation to maximize efficiency and safety.

Management Services

  • Maintenance planning and scheduling
  • Regulatory compliance management
  • Crew scheduling and management
  • Fuel management and optimization
  • Insurance and documentation handling
  • 24/7 operational support

Benefits

By entrusting your aircraft management to us, you benefit from reduced operational costs, improved aircraft utilization, and peace of mind knowing that all aspects of your aircraft operation are handled by experts.

About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and the team behind Skyward Aviation

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by aviation industry veterans, Skyward Aviation began as a small maintenance facility with a big vision: to provide exceptional aircraft services with uncompromising safety standards.

Over the years, we've grown into a full-service aviation company with facilities spanning three hangars and a team of over 50 certified professionals. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for aircraft owners and operators worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, reliable, and efficient aircraft services that exceed our clients' expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Nothing is more important than the safety of our clients, team, and the flying public.
  • Quality Excellence: We're committed to delivering work that meets or exceeds industry standards.
  • Customer Focus: We build lasting relationships by understanding and responding to our clients' needs.
  • Innovation: We continuously invest in technology and training to improve our services.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.
